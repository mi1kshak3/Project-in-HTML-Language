FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

  1. I just placed an order, can I cancel it?
    2. Unfortunately, we are unable to cancel an order once order is been placed

  2. I only received one of the items in my order, where is the rest?
    3. If you have only received part of your order, don’t worry, you’ll receive tracking on your other goods as they might be delayed.

  3. I didn't receive a confirmation email, did my order go through?
    4. Order confirmations sometimes can be delayed. Please check your spam.

  4. Why isn't my order showing up on the tracking page?
    5. Your order might not have been shipped, please allow 3-5 business days for this information to appear.

  5. How do I contact customer service, how long will it take them to respond?
    6. You can contact a us on twitter during 9 - 6PM EST. For E-Mail, expect a response within 24-48 hours.

  6. Which countries do you ship to?
    7. We currently only ship within Canada.

  7. Can I pick up my online order at the store?
    8. Sorry, at this time we are an e-commerce business and soon will be opening a store.
